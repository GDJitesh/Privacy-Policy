1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. We prioritize your privacy and ensure compliance with regulations such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy Directive (ePD).

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data directly through our app. The only data collected is for advertising purposes, which is managed by third-party services like AdMob.

3. Advertising

Our app uses AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect data to show relevant ads based on your interests. This includes cookies, device identifiers, and IP addresses. For more details on how AdMob handles your data, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.

If we use other third-party services, they will be integrated through AdMob’s mediation platform, which handles the sharing of necessary data.

4. User Control

You have the option to control the type of ads you see by managing your consent for personalized ads. We use Google's UMP (User Messaging Platform) for this purpose. You will be presented with a consent form to opt-in or opt-out of personalized ads based on your location.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect any data from children.
